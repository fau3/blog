> This article is translated from the Chinese original.

What is worth the sea turning blue for

What is worth the sea growing old for

Too many passing clouds and drifting smoke

Including you

Including me

What is worth the sea aching for

Too much knowledge

Has filled the sea with bitterness

And turned it into

Something like a giant sieve

What is worth it turning blue for

What is worth it surging for

The seawater loosens its fingers

There is only forgetting, only passing through

What is worth the sea holding on to
