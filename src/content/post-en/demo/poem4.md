> This article is translated from the Chinese original.

The first person looks up at the tree

There are birds, or there are not

In truth he is only looking at his own mood

The second person builds his home upon his wife's face

The third person, whatever he does

Always has the sound of coins ringing in his joints

The fourth person is always preparing to cry

Preparing to be moved, to be comforted

His handkerchief

Is kept in his trouser pocket

The fifth person imitates someone's tone and gestures

The difficulty of staying alive is this:

He cannot learn the way that person spits

The sixth person has just ended his first love

He has only one hand, one leg, and half a head left

The seventh person became a man of the upper class

And

From that day on, his nasal voice grew heavy

The eighth person is fighting with the ninth

The tenth person hides behind a mask

Until he dies

We never see his face
